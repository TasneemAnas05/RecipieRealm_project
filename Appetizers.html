Tabouli










    Instructions:

    Prepare Bulgur:

  1. Place the bulgur in a heatproof bowl. Pour the boiling water over the bulgur and cover the bowl with a lid or plastic wrap. Let it sit for about 20-30 minutes or until the bulgur is tender and has absorbed the water.

    2. Fluff and Cool Bulgur:

  2. Fluff the bulgur with a fork to separate the grains.
  3. Allow it to cool to room temperature.

    4. Chop Vegetables and Herbs:

  4. In a large mixing bowl, combine the finely chopped parsley, diced tomatoes, chopped mint, chopped green onions, and chopped cucumber.

    5. Add Bulgur:

  5. Add the cooled and fluffed bulgur to the bowl with the vegetables and herbs.

    6. Prepare Dressing:

  6. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, fresh lemon juice, salt, and pepper.

    7. Combine and Toss:

  7. Pour the dressing over the bulgur mixture.
  8. Gently toss the ingredients together until everything is well combined.

    9. Chill and Serve:

  9. Refrigerate the Tabbouleh for at least 1-2 hours before serving to allow the flavors to meld.
