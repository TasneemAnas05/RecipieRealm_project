About Recipe Realm :

Description :

Recipe Realm provides a range of simple-to-follow recipes from across the globe, acting as your gateway to international flavors. Whether you're new to cooking or an experienced chef ,try experimenting with a variety of recipes and ingredients, from comforting classics to unique innovations. It's a central location for food enthusiasts to share mouthwatering experiences and find inspiration for cooking with easy-to-follow directions."

Why we choose it?

Our website is a versatile culinary education platform, dedicated to teaching easy and quick cooking methods using available ingredients. With step-by-step tutorials, we guide individuals in mastering the art of cooking, offering not only traditional recipes but also a repository of new and exciting culinary creations. Beyond just recipes, it's a space for a community of cooking enthusiasts to exchange experiences, tips, and stories. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced chef, our website is a hub for an enjoyable journey of learning and experimentation in the world of cooking.

About team :

At Applied Sciences University, our team comprises three dedicated female students: Eman, Tasnim, and Jood. Not only do we share the same residence, but we've also taken our collaboration to the kitchen. We've seamlessly divided cooking times among us, discovering that our shared passion for cooking is not only a delightful way to bond but also an avenue for culinary exploration. As we navigate our academic journeys, our shared commitment to both our studies and the culinary arts has created a unique and supportive living and learning environment.
